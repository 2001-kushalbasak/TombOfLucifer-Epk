Band Bio

Tomb of Lucifer is an Oriental Blackened Death Metal band from Kolkata, India. They started their journey back in 2015 as an Old School Blackened Death Metal, They released their first EP "Blood Birth of Lucifer" back in 26th June,2016 with previous line up were Souradeep Bhattacharya on Vocals, Ayan Ghosh and Mainak Roy on Guitars and Subhajit Ghosh on Drums. Their songs mainly present the forbidden history, occultism and the dark side of the Bible which is hidden from mankind. On 26th June, 2016 For the first time Bass Baban Suman played Bass as a featured artist at the gig organized by the band on the day of release. After some time he serves his duty full time as Bassist and assists the main vocalist with harsh vocals. But with time the band dealt with some major changes in lineup to carry forward the Legacy to spread the wisdom of Lucifer. Gradually Mainak, Subhajit and Souradeep left the band for some personal reasons and Dipanjan on Guitars and Samantak on Drums fill the void within a short amount of time. Suman takes incharge the main vocal as well as Bass. Ayan with his guitar remains in the band like the pillar of the tomb.. After 7 years from the first EP the band released their full length concept album "Ode to Judah '' with the current lineup. This saga begins with the death of David leaving the entire estate in the name of a 12-year-old child. It was in the hands of that kid to create a destiny or volute in the wind of time. He searched and found the knowledge from the enlightened light which never ends Lucifer himself. While providing the knowledge, Northern Star, creator of Akephalos, questioned him about his house, The House of David. It all started with a question and ended up with the knowledge of eternity. By the time the soundscape of the band was evolving and changing into Oriental Blackened Death Metal. The four-member band is an overall balanced act between heavy distorted guttural lows and extreme long dynamical screams alongside Jewish traditional cantor and synagogue singing and demonic chants, equally fast aggressive drumming, chugging riffs with tremolo picking, fast and extreme tempo and use of complicated time signature. Some of Indian classical instruments like Sarod, Sitar, Flute etc. and middle eastern instruments like Duduk, lyre, frame drums etc and many different tribal instruments were use to enhance authenticity and the symphonic orchestrations with fusion melody of Indian classical and middle eastern soundscape balanced with extreme raw elements of Blackened Death Metal leads the band to a Unique sound. .
